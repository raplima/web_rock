We still need more rock images to make our database more interesting. We will be happy if you have nice pictures you don't mind sharing with us!

More examples will help us create better models that can be more reliable when trying to guess what is the rock you are holding.

Currently we can only guess between:

Basalt

Coquina

Garnet schist

Granite

Mica schist

We can increase the list if we get (hundreds of) new rock pictures.

Some references

The JavaScript implementation of the model loading and predicion were inspired in this post and this repository.

3Blue1Brown have a very nice neural networks explanation in their YouTube channel.

Computerphile also have a cool video explaining convolutional neural networks.

Most of the tools we used to train the convolutional neural networks to classify rocks were made with Keras as well as their models applications.

This works in your browser mostly because of TensorflowJS .

The original models you can chose in the Home tab came from:

MobileNetV2: Inverted Residuals and Linear Bottlenecks

Very Deep Convolutional Networks for Large-Scale Image Recognition (VGG)
